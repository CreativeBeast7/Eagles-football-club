About

Founded in 2012, EagleFC is a passionate and dedicated football club based in Vizhinjam. Over the years, we have risen to become a formidable team in the local football scene. With an unyielding spirit and a commitment to excellence, we have proudly claimed victory in 5 major tournaments and secured runner-up positions in 4 others.

Our journey is driven by teamwork, discipline, and a shared love for the game. As we continue to grow, we remain focused on achieving greater heights, always staying true to our core values of sportsmanship and community.

Join us as we soar to new challenges and victories!

Honours

Champions

EagleFC has proudly claimed 5 championship titles, with one in 2019, two in 2020, and two more in 2021. These victories reflect our team's consistent growth and dominance on the field over the years.

Runner-up

EagleFC has also been runner-up in 4 tournaments, once in 2020 and three times in 2021. These close finishes further highlight our team's competitive spirit and determination.

Born to Win

At EagleFC, we firmly believe in trusting the process, knowing that consistent effort and dedication lead to success. Every challenge we face strengthens our resolve, and we are confident that our hard work will continue to yield great results.
