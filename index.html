About Eagle FC

Founded in 2012, EagleFC is a passionate and dedicated football club based in Vizhinjam. Over the years, we have risen to become a formidable team in the local football scene. With an unyielding spirit and a commitment to excellence, we have proudly claimed victory in 5 major tournaments and secured runner-up positions in 4 others. Our journey is driven by teamwork, discipline, and a shared love for the game. As we continue to grow, we remain focused on achieving greater heights, always staying true to our core values of sportsmanship and community. Join us as we soar to new challenges and victories!
