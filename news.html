About our Village

Vizhinjam

Vizhinjam is a coastal town located in the southern part of Kerala, known for its scenic beauty and vibrant fishing community. It has a rich maritime history and is home to the Vizhinjam International Seaport, which is set to become a major hub for global trade. The town’s picturesque beaches and cultural heritage make it a unique blend of tradition and modern development....

Our Lady Of Good Voyage

Vizhinjam Church, officially known as Our Lady of Good Voyage Church, is a prominent spiritual landmark in the coastal town. With its striking architecture and deep-rooted history, it has been a center of faith and community for local fishermen and residents for generations. The church is not only a place of worship but also plays a vital role in the cultural and social life of Vizhinjam....

Vizhinjam Old Portuguese Church

The Old Portuguese Church in Vizhinjam, also known as the Vizhinjam St. Mary’s Church, is a historic structure that dates back to the colonial era. Built by the Portuguese in the 16th century, it stands as a symbol of the region’s rich Christian heritage and European influence. Though weathered by time, the church remains an important religious and historical site, reflecting the cultural exchange between Portugal and Kerala....
