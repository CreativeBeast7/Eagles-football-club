Our Honours

Celebrating our journey and achievements on the field

Champions

EagleFC has proudly claimed 5 championship titles, with one in 2019, two in 2020, and two more in 2021. These victories reflect our team's consistent growth and dominance on the field over the years.

Runner-up

We have also finished as runners-up in 4 tournaments, once in 2020 and three times in 2021. These close finishes highlight our team's competitive spirit and determination.

Born to Win

At EagleFC, we believe in trusting the process. Consistent effort and dedication lead to success. Every challenge we face strengthens our resolve, and we are confident that our hard work will continue to yield great results.
